About CampusLife:

CampusLife is the website covering college life, sports, and tips writen by and for college students. We currently only operate out of Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, however if you want to have your school represented click here.

Contact CampusLife:

To contact the CampusLife team, please either email us CampusLife@outlook.com, or tweet at us @CampusLife.

Add Your College:

CampusLife relies upon inspiring and fantastic students from colleges around the world, if you want content pertaining to your school, make sure to email us CampusLife@outlook.com and describe your school in the body of the email with at least one link to your school's website. We will review the information and then reach out to you when your school can be added!

We suggest that you either coordinate with the school president/dean(s) so that the school can have a coordinated team to handle content that will be added to CampusLife.
