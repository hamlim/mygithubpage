On Blogging

How I want to change my blogging habits

By: Matt Hamlin

: October 18th, 2016

This is my 54th blog post on this domain, thats quite amazing that I have made so many blog posts on here in such a short span. If you have read some of my blog posts you will know I tend to write about a mixture of personal things and some development things as well. I have recieved a ton of feedback about my blog posts so far, however most of the feedback has been for or from my personal blog posts. I started to think why that would be and then I happened to listen to a Shop Talk podcast where they talk about seperating blogging across two domains. One domain acts as your digital entity, where you blog about personal aspects of your life and the other domain acts as the experimental one, where you can blog about the technical things you enjoy. For me this would mean moving many of my Web Development posts over to a new domain.

I thought about this for a while, realizing how much time and effort it would take to shift this blog over to two seperate entities, but I decided I wanted to go through with it, even if I don't tend to blog much about either topic I wanted to make it easier for those who read my posts to filter the content they read from me into these categories. The move won't be sudden, I will be slowly porting over many of my development focused posts to Goexploring.today/WorldWideWeb, where I blog about web development and other web technology content.
