Monday Mornings

: November 7th, 2016

With Daylight Savings ending this weekend, it feels like I am rolling in to work now far later than I should be because of how light it is outside. I normally get in right around 6:44am, when there are only a handful of people in the office at all, let alone on my floor. I mostly finsh up some of the work I was doing in the morning before leaving to work, such as finish writing blog posts like this.

I currently put in about 9 and a half hours of work each workday and it feels pretty tiring, I wish I could work on my own side projects here and there during the workday to reinvigorate my creativity for work and for my own projects.

Either way people are starting to show up so time for me to start getting back to my work. Let me know if you like these kind of stream-of-consciousness posts or not. Sometimes I feel like I ramble on and get sidetracked way too often for these kind of posts to actually make sense to anyone else.
