A rundown of my October.

: November 1st, 2016

This past month has gone by really quickly, at work I shifted from one large project to yet another large project, and outside of work I started many more side projects and worked on building out my website into a better format and layout.

I have been thinking a lot about building out a better blogging system, covering things like site layout, content, and mindset about blogging. I definitely want to get into blogging about more personal things, as well as blogging about more serious topics as well. I have a ton of ideas that I want to explore writing about, most of them technical but I also want to explore other topics.

I also added comments to most of my blog posts published last month and I have yet to get a single comment on any post. I think I am going to stop putting the comment thread at the bottom of each post mainly because its not adding anything at all to the posts for future readers let alone actually providing more feedback to me about the posts.

I have also been posting more content than ever last month with 21 blog posts published within 31 days, besting the previous month by several posts. I feel like I can keep this momentun going forward into the holidays as well.

I also somehow feel like I really dropped in productivity on making web doodads though, I haven't really made that much in terms of solid web sites or web applications on anything off of this domain. I aim to fix that by setting a goal to make a redesigned website once a week for the next 5 weeks on my new project WorldWideWeb. So if you have any great ideas for a site to be redesigned hit me up on Twitter or on Snapchat, or anywhere with your ideas.

So here is to November, hopefully I am as productive in terms of content generation and also in terms of project development.
