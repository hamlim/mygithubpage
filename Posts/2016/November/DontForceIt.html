Today I was trying to force out an article that I really wanted to write, talking about a subject that is pretty relevant. But I sat there trying to push out the paragraphs one after another but couldn't go any farther than one or two paragraphs.

The problem is that I felt like I had this pressure to get the article out by the end of the day because of my momentum, or because the idea was still really fresh in my head.

Don't force an idea because it is fresh in your mind.

The worst problem is that the idea was too fresh. I was too eager to actually put it down in a file and publish it instead of thinking through it first and coming up with a better flow to present the idea.

The point is that you shouldn't really force an idea into something before you have a good grasp on it.
