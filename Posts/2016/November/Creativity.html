Creativity

: November 6th, 2016

For the past week I have been trying to get my creativity juices flowing by working on different small little projects, things I can get done in the span of about an hour or two. Most of these ideas were just small random UI/UX thoughts I wanted to work on, one of them was working on styling of a link with a nice animated background color change on hover, another one was to experiment with SVG and styling SVG with CSS.

There is a small part of my mind thats screaming at me for not working on something significant, but at the moment I feel dried out of drive or momentum to work on any significant projects. I have this list of things I want to work on but it feels like I just keep on piling on more requirements as time goes on.

Right now it feels like work is sucking all the energy and creativity out of me because I am forced to build things with none of the new technologies, or because I am also in clean up mode, cleaning up old files across our code base and making sure things still work correctly and look correct.

I am looking forward to my vacation time in two months so I can jump into an exciting new project, something a bit more meaty than just little demos.

Have you struggled with keeping your creative juices flowing outside of work? If you have any tips I would love to know them!
