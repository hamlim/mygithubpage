Sunday Morning Recap

A quick recap of some interesting content I have stumbled upon.

: October 30rd, 2016

Good Morning! I am currently huddled over a warm cup of coffee mixed with some delicious almond milk and a bowl of oatmeal this beautiful Sunday morning. This past week was filled with product announcements and also some sad news as well.

1.

This week Microsoft held a huge Windows and hardware event on Wednesday, the largest announcement of that event was their new desktop PC Surface Studio. You can see the announcement video below. They also announced a ton of 3D-focused features in a new Windows 10 update coming later this year, as well as a new Surface Book that only bumps the CPU and GPU.

2.

Microsoft wasn't the only large company to hold a hardware event this week however, Apple also held a hardware event where they unveiled three new Macbook Pros two of which have a new touch bar replacing the function keys at the top of the keyboard. You can find the marketing video for these new laptops below as well. Owen Williams posted a great essay about how Apple doesn't know who they are making the Macbook line of computers for anymore, and is definitely worth a read.

I will say however that I definitely want one with a touchbar (I think), just not sure on which model I want.

3.

On the lighter side of things, with Halloween right around the corner tomorrow this video of a dog's owner dressing up as the dog's favorite toy went pretty viral within the past few days.

4.

In the spirit of Halloween some cool person made this awesome VR web experience that you should totally open up on your phone to experience. You can find this experience here.
