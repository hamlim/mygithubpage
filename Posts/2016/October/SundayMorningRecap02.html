Sunday Morning Recap

A quick recap of some interesting content I have stumbled upon.

: October 23rd, 2016

Good Morning! I am currently huddled over a warm cup of coffee mixed with some delicious almond milk and a bowl of oatmeal this beautiful Sunday morning. Friday and Saturday were both pretty gloomy overcast days, so I am glad that there is some sun this morning.

1.

For some reason I stumbled upon this pretty old blog post from Zach Holman who builds upon his blog post where he talks about being fired from Github and offers a really interesting read on how companies, employees and friends should handle the firing process. It is definitely worth a read if you want to enjoy some humor and also some insight this morning.

2.

I also found this really cool web application where you can make music using your keyboard, mouse and some sliders, however the really cool part about this is this specific track that someone created and I found which is actually quite an interesting listen. You can listen to it here, however you should know that it is very electronic sounding, so if that is right up your alley I hope you enjoy it!

3.

The trailer for the movie Logan was released earlier this week, it seems like a much more interesting X-Men-Universe movie than the ones I remember watching early on. You can watch the trailer below.

4.

If you happened to be asleep all day Friday then you missed one of the largest Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks in the US ever. Some attackers were able to build a network of thousands or more zombie processors (mostly internet of things devices like your smart sprinkler system) to take down one of the largest DNS service providers in the US that held the DNS records of large sites like Twitter, Netflix, Spotify and many more. Ars Technica has a pretty good short read on the subject that I suggest going through. NPR has an excellent read about it as well.

5.

Finally this morning I leave you with a really great podcast episode wherein a few people dive into some of their (and popular) theories about the breakout show Westworld on HBO. There are spoilers within the episode so I highly suggest at least watching the first three episodes before listening to the episode. You can find the podcast here on iTunes or here on Google Play I personally have really been enjoying the show and really wished that it had been released like Netflix shows where all the episodes become available at the same time.

So there is my Sunday Morning Recap, I hope you enjoyed it, feel free to chime in below with some interesting content you found this past week!
